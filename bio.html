Biography


I was born in 1987 in Riga, Latvia. I started my training in 2006, and by 2008 I’ve joined the Riga’s Circus Artists Association. My trainer - Irina Levit, an international award winner in the sport of acrobatics, has helped me the most to become the artist that I am today.


As you can see from my pictures and videos, my specialties are Contortionists and Frontbending act (flexibility). I’m optimistic, hardworking and a friendly person.


Valery Komisarenko

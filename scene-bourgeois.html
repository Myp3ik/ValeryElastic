Bourgeois



There is nothing more alluring than the taste of freedom in France. Meet a tipsy French man, who dreams of high society, but despises it at the same time. A freethinker, a libertine living his life on the edge in cabarets and pubs, enjoying women, booze and the charm of French chanson.
